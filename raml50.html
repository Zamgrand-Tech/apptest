Matakan Koyon Ramli

Wannan littafi kunshi matakan koyon Ilimin Ramli guda 50, domin yan koyo da wadanda suke son karin gogewa a wannan Ilimi na Ramli (geomancy). Kamar yadda muka sani Ramli yaqunshi digo wadanda ake digawa har a fidda taurari 16 (Ashkal) daga nan kuma se a fara hukunci.

● Ana iya gane abinda ya faru a baya, da wanda ke faruwa yanzu, da wanda ze faru nan gaba da izinin Allah.

● Annabi Idris yana daga cikin Annabawan da Allah yabaiwa wannan ilimi. Ababin mu’ujiza shine wanda Annabi Muhammad (s.a.w) yai nuni dashi a cikin Hadisi ingantacce cikin Sahihu Muslim cewa:
“ءﺎﯾﺑﻧﻻا نﻣ ﻲﺑﻧ نﺎﻛ كاذﻓ ﮫطﺧ قﻓاو نﻣو طﺧﯾ”

Sabida haka wannan ilimi tsohone kuma halaktacce. Allah ya bamu ikon fahimtarsa dakuma amfani da shi. Wassalam.
