Izanazala

Wannan littafi mai Albarka ya kawo hukunci na izanazala, shin fassarar taurari a cikin gidaje kamar idan kausaji ya sauka a 1 ko 2 har zuwa gidan 16 me yake nufi cikin wadannan gidaje haka za abi taurarin har zuwa kan jama ‘a tauraro na sha shidda 16 kowanne mene fassararsa in ya sauka a kowanne gida.
دﻣﺣﻣ لاءو دﻣﺣﻣ ﻰﻠﻋ لﺻ مﮭﻠﻟا

Sadaukarwa

Ina sadaukar da wannan littafi ga Manzon Allah s.a.w da Ahalinsa dakuma Ruhin iyayena Alh Ibrahim Rabi’u lalo Dambatta da Haj Safiyya Abubakar Dambatta Allah kai musu Rahama in Tamu tazo kasa mu cika da Imani Ameen.

Godiya

Ina mai godiya da fatan Alkhairi ga daukacin malamaina baki daya na addini dana zamani dama duk wanda ya sanar dani koda harafine Allah ya saka musu da alkhairi, musamman wadanda suka koyar dani wannan fannin na ilimin Ramli da Hisabi kamar:

1. Sheikh Sharif Nasir Sahrif Ali (Hakimin laqani na gwale yamma)
2. Sheikh Jibril Sani (Sarkin Laqani na gwale lga)
3. Sheikh Alhasan Yamadawa (ﻲﺧوطﻟا بﺣﺎﺻ)
4. Sheikh Dr Abubakar Gaida (ﻲﺧوطﻟا بﺣﺎﺻ)
5. Sheikh Sani Abacha youth centre.
