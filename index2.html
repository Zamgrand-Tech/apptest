Adamu Ibrahim Alfalakey, mazaunin Kano, shaharren malamin addinin Musulunci, an haife shi a Sharada, Malam Kano, Nigeria a shekarar 1990. Ya yi karatun Diploma a Aminu Kano College of Islamic and Legal Studies (AKCILS) a fannin Education English & Islamic Studies. Ya yi digiri (Degree) a Bayero University (B.U.K) a fannin B.A (Ed) Islamic Studies. Haka kuma, ya yi karatu a Federal University Dutsin-Ma (FUDMA), inda ya karanci B.A Islamic Studies.
