Ayyukan da Mukeyi

Munayin ayyuka na roqon Allah kowanne iri domin neman biyan buqata kamar:- karya asiri, tambaya, Ibdali, dafa’i, tsari, mahabba, kwarjini, farin jini, neman mijiko mata, maganin aljanu, maganin mayu, kambun baka, ido, tsafi, maganin kasuwa da jama’a. Daurin baki, mallakar miji ko mata, promotion, jarrabawa, rinjaye, mallakar hadumi, Dama biyan kowace irin buqata bi’iznillahi
